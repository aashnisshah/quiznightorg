Virtual Quiz Night Fundraiser

COVID-19 has affected us all in many ways. Since we all need to be home before 9:00pm, why not take part in a virtual quiz night and help raise some funds for folks in need.

This quiz night event is hosted in partnership with Lions Nairobi Greater Club AND Lions Nairobi Phoenix Club.

All funds raised will go to the Baby Remand Home, a home to give children a chance at a better future.
